We believe in connecting People with their perfect companions

Purpose

Here at My Perfect Pet, we understand that pets make the world a brighter place. We also understand that having a pet that matches your personality and lifestyle is very important. There are millions of animals looking for their forever home, and our goal is to match them with the perfect person!

How it works

We offer several different ways to connect people and pets every day. You can start by taking the My Perfect Pet Survey, which will recommend what kind of pet would be best based on your personality and lifestyle.

You can also search for your perfect pet by location and/or pet type. You'll be able to search through the profiles of adoptable pets, and be one step closer to giving your perfect pet a forever home.
